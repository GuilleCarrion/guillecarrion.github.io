In this paper we study the cellularization of classifying spaces of saturated fusion systems with respect to classifying spaces of finite p-groups. We give explicit algebraic criteria to decide when a classifying space is cellular. Moreover, we describe computations for a given family of exotic examples introduced by Díaz-Ruiz-Viruel.
